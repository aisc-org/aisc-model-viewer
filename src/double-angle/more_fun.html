This connection can be found in the real world in Neyland Stadium. Check it out at 35°57'19.8"N 83°55'26.9"W. Go Vols!

The column is a W24x##. You won't find this section in the 15th Edition of the steel manual since it is no longer commonly produced. If you are looking for information on historical shapes AISC's Design Guide ## is a good place to start.

The work point of a connection is where the center line of the connected members meet. It is common for this to be at the top of the base plate.
